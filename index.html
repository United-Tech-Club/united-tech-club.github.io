About Us

United Tech Club is a student-led organization committed to fostering innovation and technological advancement. We organize a range of events and workshops, including hackathons, that provide students with opportunities to explore cutting-edge technologies, collaborate on innovative projects, and develop practical skills. Our mission is to create a vibrant community where students can learn, grow, and contribute to the ever-evolving world of technology.

Founded

This club was founded on July 2021 by students of United Technical College and has been run by them ever since.

Our Mission

At United Tech Club, our mission is to inspire and empower students to explore, innovate, and excel in the field of technology. We strive to create an inclusive platform where students can engage with the latest technological advancements through hands-on experiences, collaborative projects, and knowledge-sharing events. By organizing hackathons, workshops, and other tech-driven initiatives, we aim to nurture the next generation of innovators and equip them with the skills and mindset needed to succeed in a rapidly evolving digital world.

Team

Our dedicated team of passionate students works tirelessly to organize and deliver impactful tech events and workshops, driving innovation and collaboration across the community.

Board

The United Tech Club Board oversees strategic planning and ensures that our initiatives align with our mission to foster technological growth and student engagement.

Media

For media inquiries, event coverage, or partnership opportunities, our press team is here to provide information and support to help share our story.
